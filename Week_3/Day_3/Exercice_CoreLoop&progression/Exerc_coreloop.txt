4Step Progression (Teach / Test / Twist / Master)

New Mechanic: Water Dash (fast movement through fire zones)

Teach : 
A safe area introduces Water Dash with no enemies and a clear visual trail to follow.

Test : 
Short fire corridors require using Water Dash to cross safely before water drains.

Twist : 
Enemies and moving fire walls force the player to dash with timing and positioning.

Master : 
A boss fight demands chaining Water Dash with attacks to avoid lethal fire waves and win.
