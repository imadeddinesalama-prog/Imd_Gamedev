Goal : 

Save the other nations from Fire Nation control and take revenge for the destruction of the water world.

Rules : 

1- Each nation is protected by Fire Nation outposts that must be cleared to free it.

2 - Water energy is limited and must be restored by saving villages or sacred springs.


Feedback : 

- Success Feedback:
Chains of fire shatter, the nation’s colors return, and a powerful water surge sound plays.

- Failure Feedback:
The screen heats up with red distortion, the slime steams, and a sharp burn sound is heard.


Cosmetic Reward 

Reward: Nation Marks
After saving a nation, a glowing symbol appears inside the slime, changing its color accents and ripple effects—purely cosmetic, a badge of revenge and victory.
