Accessibility Features

1- Colorblind-Friendly Indicators

* Where: Fire zones and water sources use distinct shapes and patterns in addition to color (e.g., stripes on fire, ripple pattern on water).

2- Adjustable Text Size for Prompts

* Where: Dialogue boxes, tutorial hints, and skill descriptions can be resized in the settings menu.


In-Game Prompt 

"Your water energy is low! Find a nearby spring to refill before entering the fire zone."

This prompt appears just before the player enters a high-damage fire area, giving beginners a clear warning without breaking immersion.
